FAQ

Frequently Asked Questions

What is Currenzy Exchange?

Currenzy Exchange is a platform that allows users to convert currencies easily and efficiently.

How do I convert currencies on Currenzy Exchange?

Simply select the currencies you want to convert from and to, enter the amount, and click the convert button. The latest exchange rate will be used to calculate your result.

Are the exchange rates updated in real time?

Yes, our platform fetches the latest exchange rates from trusted financial sources to ensure accurate conversions.

Do I need to create an account to use Currenzy Exchange?

No account is required for basic currency conversions. However, creating an account allows you to save your conversion history and access additional features.
